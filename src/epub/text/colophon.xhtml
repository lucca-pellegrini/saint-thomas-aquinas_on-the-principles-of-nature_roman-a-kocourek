Colophon

On the Principles of Nature, to Brother Sylvester
was published in 1255 by
Saint Thomas Aquinas.
It was translated from Latin in 1951 by
Roman A. Kocourek.

This ebook was produced by
Lucca Pellegrini,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2010 by
Fr. Joseph Kenny, O.P.
for
The Dominican House of Studies in Washington, D.C

The cover page is adapted from
A Stained Glass Window of Thomas Aquinas in St. Joseph’s Catholic Church (Central City, Kentucky),
a picture taken in 2014 by
Nheyob.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.

The first edition of this ebook was released on
January 1, 1900, 12:00 a.m.
